Understanding the Metrics

Operational metrics are unique to each company, unlike universal financial metrics like Earnings Per Share or P/E Ratio. Operational metrics are the particular factors that drive a company’s performance specifically, such as iPhone sales for Apple or hours viewed for Netflix.
